Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is MsPan

MsPan is an online language-learning platform that connects English language learners in China with qualified native American English teachers for one-on-one sessions through live video chat.

Where do I teach

Teachers can teach from anywhere with a computer and internet access.

Application/Requirement

How do I apply to teach on MsPan?

Please sign up and start your application here_______(link).

Do I have to be a university student to apply the job?

Yes. You need to either be a currently enrolled university student in any field(undergraduate, graduate, or PHD student), or you hold a bachelor degree or above and with ESL teaching experience.

Do I need teaching experience?

We welcome teachers from all fields of study and backgrounds, however teaching or tutoring experience is strongly recommended and in most cases is required. Exceptions are made on a case-by-case basis.

Do I need a teaching certificate?

Any type of formal teaching certificate is preferable and will increase your chances of admission, but is not necessarily required. We are currently hiring certified ESL teachers or university students who study in this field like TESOL/ESL.

Do I need to know Chinese language to conduct my lesson?

No. The lessons are designed to be an immersion experience for your students. However, understanding or speaking some Chinese will definitely ease the communication and explanation especially in the beginning between you and your student.

What equipment do I need?

1) High-speed internet.
2) A desktop or a laptop with a webcam.
3) A headset with microphone.

What is the hiring process?

1. Sign up and submit your application form.
2. Interview + demo lesson.
3. Complete your teacher profile + submit required materials.
4. Match with your students.
5. Begin teaching!

Interview

Where does the interview happen?

The interview will be conducted online through our website, and all you need to do is to login to your account and book a time slot, and have your interview with us on the designated date that you have chosen. Make sure you test your equipment beforehand, as we are also assessing your equipment to see if your internet connection is able to maintain a 30-min smooth connection with a server in China.

What equipment do I need?

1) High-speed internet
2) A desktop or a laptop with a webcam
3) A headset with microphone

How long is the entire interview and what would be the format?

The entire interview will last 20-25 minutes. The interview has two parts.
Part I. (10-15 minutes) One-on-one Interview
Part II. (5-10 minutes) Teaching Demo

Why do I have to do a teaching demo?

The online lessons we offered to students are often around 40 minutes of which contains two parts, lecturing, and interaction with students. In the teaching section, you will have 5 to 10 minutes to present what you have prepared for your students. The time is so short that you need a good plan in order to make every minute effective. For the interaction part, it is highly important for you as a teacher to motivate and encourage your students to speak up and talk more in complete sentences, to express their ideas in cohered and logical ways, to use the expression and reference of American culture and ultimately build up their confidence of talking in English. This way ensures high quality teaching from you as teachers that helps students improve.

What will I be evaluated on my teaching demo?

You will be evaluated on how well you are able to explain and teach the content to different level ESL students, your ability to scaffold and engage the student in a conversation/discussion, your ability to motivate and encourage the student to speak in a more confident and comfortable way, your ESL teaching skills and your time management skills.

How can I prepare for a teaching demo?

Detailed breakdown can facilitate your preparation. You may use the following structure to help you prepare your demo:
1. (5 minutes) Teaching presentation
2. (5 minutes) Interaction with interviewers
please treat us as your students and encourage us to engage in a discussion with you.

What topics or content can I choose for my demo?

You will be provided a list of interesting topics (ranging from food, campus life and traveling) from one of our course package. You can choose from any one of the topics that is on the list.

What are some things I can work on to improve easy-to-follow for ESL leaners?

Simplicity;
Personal connection;
Articulated delivery;
Facial expression;
Dynamic tones, pitches, and volumes;
Inviting attitude;
Showing personal attention

Begin Teaching

How many students do I teach in each class?

All MsPan classes are one-on-one private sessions at this point.

How long is each class?

Each class is guaranteed 45 minutes of active teaching time. After that, teachers may take a few minutes to leave comments. The total time commitment is 50 minutes per class, not including preparation time. 3 minutes are allowed for possible tech problems before a class is cancelled.

Do I need to prepare teaching materials?

Yes. We have some teaching materials recommended to teachers, and teacher are encouraged to prepare teaching materials as well.

Do teachers always teach the same students?

No. Teachers will be matched with different students when they could build a long-term relationship, and they will also teach any student who books their class.

What video technology do I use to teach?

All teaching and learning on MsPan is done through Zoom (don’t worry if you are new to Zoom. It’s very easy to get started and easy to use).

How many private sessions can I teach?

Teachers can teach an unlimited amount of private sessions on MsPan.

How does scheduling work?

Teachers must enter their weekly availability one month in advance. Teachers’ weekly schedules are sent out each Saturday at 10AM Beijing time. Teachers can be booked for classes during the week, no less than 24 hours in advance. MsPan encourages all teachers to check their emails and their schedule in the system daily.

What hours should teachers be available to teach?

Due to the 12-hour time difference, MsPan recommend teachers to schedule their lessons during the peak hours. Peak hours refer to classes taught between 6-10pm Beijing time every evening, as well as 9-11am on Saturday and Sunday. This is the best time to be available as it drastically increases the likelihood of a class being booked by the student. Summer peak hours (for approximately July and August) will be: 9am-11am and 6pm-10pm Beijing time.
