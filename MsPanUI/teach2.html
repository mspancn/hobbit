what we offer

MsPan offers Chinese students with opportunities to learn from English native speakers from North American through our online platform. The platform makes it possible for English language learners in China who have no access to English native speakers with a real learning experience with native English speaking teachers.

What we believe

MsPan allows students all over China to have access to native English speakers, teachers and tutors. Students can learn and practice LCL skills from trained tutors with professionally developed education products.

real conversation

We believe that it is crucial for language learners to really see the purpose and benefits of using the language they learned in books into the real world by interacting with native speakers so that they gain confidence and self-motivation.

We believe that a better way to master speaking a foreign language is through constant and meaningful practice with the native speakers. We believe with the right way and practice everyone can master a foreign language. We believe that as teachers you are the keys to help making all this to happen.

Why MsPan

Anytime. Anywhere.

You can earn money from the comfort of your home while experiencing some of the most rewarding moments of your life by receiving sincere appreciations from your students. That is why you choose us a passionate and dedicated education group who truly cares about their students, and you will be joining us to help them grow as their mentor.

MsPan offers an newly developed online technology platform, where Chinese students can visit and find tutors. On the front end, students can schedule online learning sessions with tutors through MsPan Bookme system, and connect with tutors through MsPan Chatroom virtually to practice English with Tutor. On the back end, MsPan platform provides services on efficient transaction management, teachers/tutors management, appointment management, students management and billing management to streamline the workflow and minimize the maintenance cost.
