what we offer

MsPan allows students all over China to have access to native English speakers, teachers and tutors. Students can learn and practice language, cultural adaptation, and learning skills from trained tutors with professionally developed education products.

What we believe

real conversation

We believe that it is crucial for language learners to really see the purpose and benefits of using the language they learned in books into the real world by interacting with native speakers so that they gain confidence and self-motivation.

We believe that a better way to master speaking a foreign language is through constant and meaningful practice with the native speakers. We believe with the right way and practice everyone can master a foreign language. We believe that as teachers you are the keys to help making all this to happen.

English Tutors Wanted

We are hiring!

Job requirements

1. Over 18 years old, and U.S. citizens or permanent residents, who are eligible to work in the U.S.

2. Currently full-time university student (undergraduate, graduate, or PHD student), or hold a bachelor degree or above and with ESL teaching experience.

3. Native English proficiency required, from all fields of study and backgrounds, teaching or tutoring experience is strongly recommended.

Payment

Tutor's rates per 45-min session:
1. Level A: $20
2. Level B: $25
3. Level C: $30

1. Level A: Native English Speaker.
2. Level B: Native English Speaker + Mandarin Proficiency,
or
Native English Speaker + Certified English Teacher.
3. Level C: Native English Speaker + Mandarin Proficiency + Certified English Teacher.

Resources

1. MsPan provides supporting materials that are designed by professional educators. These materials contain structured topics and learning modules.
2. The materials and resources provided help tutors understand basic skills and tips necessary for how to effectively teach Chinese students to learn English as a second language.
3. Tutors should also be able to provide customized teaching service based on individual student’s personal needs.
